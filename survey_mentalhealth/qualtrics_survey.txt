<h1>Consent to Participate</h1>

<h2>Purpose of Study</h2>
<p>You are invited to participate in a research study that aims to understand how registered dietitians evaluate outputs generated by Large Language Models (LLMs) for dietitian and nutrition information. Your expertise as a registered dietitian will provide valuable insights into the accuracy and reliability of these models in performing tasks related to nutrition and dietetics.</p>

<h2>If you agree to participate:</h2>
<ul>
    <li>You will be presented with dietitian-related questions and will review two different LLM-generated responses for each question. You will vote on which output answers the question better. After each review, you will provide a 1-2 sentence explanation for your choice. We estimate that this will take approximately 1 hour of your time.</li>
    <li>You will be compensated $75 upon completion of this study.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Risks/Benefits/Confidentiality of Data</h2>
<p>There are no known risks associated with this study. While there may not be direct benefits to you, your participation will contribute to our understanding of LLM-driven evaluations in dietetics, potentially leading to improved models and applications in the future.</p>
<p>Your name and email address will be kept confidential and used only for study purposes. Only two of our listed research members will have access to the data during analysis. All data will be anonymized and securely stored.</p>

<h2>Participation or Withdrawal</h2>
<p>You have the choice to participate in this study or opt out at any moment. There will be no penalties or effect on your relationship with the University of Notre Dame if you choose not to participate or decide to leave the study at any point.</p>
